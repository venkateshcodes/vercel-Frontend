About ICRI 2026

The Inter-disciplinary conference on Research & Innovation (IDCRI) 2026 is a premier academic event organized by Sanskaram University. Now in its 5th edition, ICRI has established itself as a leading platform for researchers, academicians, and industry professionals to share groundbreaking discoveries and foster interdisciplinary collaboration.

Conference Theme

"Empowering Knowledge, Inspiring Innovation" - This year's theme emphasizes the transformative power of research in addressing global challenges. We invite contributions that bridge traditional boundaries between disciplines, creating innovative solutions for a sustainable future.

Conference Objectives

  • Provide a platform for presenting cutting-edge research
  • Foster interdisciplinary collaboration and knowledge exchange
  • Connect academia with industry for real-world impact
  • Recognize and reward excellence in research
  • Promote publication in high-impact journals

About Sanskaram University

Sanskaram University is a premier institution of higher education dedicated to academic excellence, research innovation, and holistic student development. Established with a vision to nurture future leaders, the university has consistently ranked among the top educational institutions in the country.

Our Vision

"To be a global center of excellence in education, research, and innovation, fostering responsible citizens who contribute to society's progress."

Our Mission

  • Foster research and innovation across disciplines
